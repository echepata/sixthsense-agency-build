Immersive Fine Dining Experience

Be captivated and enchanted by our bespoke and totally customized immersive multi-sensory dining experience, designed to take fine dining to the next level. We harness sight, taste, sound, touch, and scent, aiming to create and frame a compelling and alluring narrative, leading to a deeply immersive emotional connection throughout the journey.

We deliver a unique immersive three-course fine dining experience which combines technology, theatrical gastronomy and storytelling, designed to absorb and fascinate all your senses and faculties.

Prepare to be entertained and intrigued by the dynamism and energy of an unimaginable experience, superb food, unmatched beverage, together with the most qualified waitstaff providing outstanding and personalized guest experiences and a world standard end-to-end service.


A storytelling adventure

Our production plays with stories of memory and impermanence, A Journey Through Time, to remind us we are not fixed, but in a constant state of flow. We hope its artful blending of past, present and future will stay with you and remind you the principles of this wonderful life.

This experience includes the SixthSense main production, gastronomy, beverage and waitstaff; it’s fully customized, and can be brought to your preferred venue, office, yacht or private home.

Theatrical Gastronomy

At SixthSense, we believe that exquisitely delicious food nourishes the soul and each one of our senses, after all, taste is simply a construction of our mind. With magnificent food paired with stunning drinks, unique and luscious flavours and amazing presentation we create, not just excitement, but a truly interactive and engaged community of joyful customers feeling cheerful and contented whilst absorbing true immersion and personal mindfulness.
